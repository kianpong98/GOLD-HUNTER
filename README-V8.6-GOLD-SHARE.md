# V8.6 — Gold Share

Additive-only update to the independent central-bank gold reserve module.

- Adds `shareOfReservesPct` to the verified reserve snapshot.
- Shows Gold Share in featured country cards and the full country table.
- Live holdings refreshes preserve the latest verified Gold Share instead of clearing it.
- Gold Share means the market value of official gold holdings divided by total official reserves.
- Calendar, Admin, Forecast, Actual, Previous, Last Release, SPDR and quotes are unchanged.
