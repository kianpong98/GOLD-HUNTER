Which news matters most for gold?

Inflation reports, labour-market data, economic growth figures and Federal Reserve decisions can change expectations for interest rates, real yields and the U.S. dollar. These channels often influence gold volatility.

How to read the result

Compare actual with forecast, then consider revisions, the U.S. dollar and Treasury yields. A single headline number should not be used in isolation.
