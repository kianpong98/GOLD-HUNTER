Why ETF holdings matter

Gold-backed ETF holdings represent metal held to support fund shares. Persistent inflows may signal stronger investment demand, while outflows may signal weaker demand.

What to monitor

Total tonnes

The latest reported holdings.

Daily change

Whether holdings increased or decreased.

30-day trend

The broader direction rather than one isolated day.

Price confirmation

Compare holdings with gold price, yields and DXY.

Important limitation

ETF holdings are one demand indicator. They do not measure all physical, futures, options or central-bank activity.
