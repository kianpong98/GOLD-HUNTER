What is gold swing trading?

Gold swing trading aims to capture a meaningful part of a multi-session price move. Positions may remain open longer than intraday scalps, so planning, risk control and patience matter.

The Gold Hunter approach

We focus on disciplined execution, clear invalidation, high-quality setups and the larger move. The objective is not to trade every fluctuation.

Who is it for?

It is designed for gold traders who want a structured XAUUSD process and who prefer selective setups over overtrading.
